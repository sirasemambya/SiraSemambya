Round Up for Change: Partners in Caring

Project Management

While interning with Wakefern, a team of fellow interns and I crafted a charity proposal called Change For Caring. This proposal was to create a new, interesting way to earn money for Wakefern's charity called Partners in Caring. The proposal was for every cashier at Shoprite to ask each customer if they would like to round up to the nearest dollar. Our projection was the program will raise $31 million in a year. Wakefern accepted our proposal but changed the name to Round for Change. The program was implemented in February of 2017
